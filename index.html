Pikachu

Pikachu is one of the most recognized characters in the Pokémon universe and is commonly known as an Electric-type Pokémon. With its cute appearance and yellow color, it has a large fan base worldwide. Pikachu is renowned for its "electric" abilities, using these powers to gain an advantage in battles against opponents. Additionally, Pikachu has a strong bond of friendship, making it an invaluable companion for its trainers.

Bulbasaur

Bulbasaur is a Pokémon that carries both flower and plant characteristics. It is distinguished by its green body and the flower on its back. Bulbasaur lives in harmony with nature and is quite sensitive to its surroundings. Thanks to its unique abilities, it has various healing powers by connecting with plants and nature. These features make it a special Pokémon in both battles and the Pokémon training process.

Charmander

Charmander is a Fire-type Pokémon known for its connection to fire. The flame on its tail indicates its mood and health; if the flame starts to go out, it signifies its weakening. Charmander is a brave and determined Pokémon, serving as a significant source of power for its trainers in battles. Gradually evolving into a more powerful form, Charizard, makes it even more impressive.

Squirtle

Squirtle is known as a Water-type Pokémon and has the appearance of a small turtle. It can make powerful attacks using water and can incapacitate its opponents. Squirtle swims excellently underwater, and its strong connection to water gives it an advantage in many situations. Its bond with its trainers makes it a loyal friend.

Meowth

Meowth is commonly known as a cat-like Pokémon and possesses both Normal and Dark-type characteristics. Meowth is smarter than many other Pokémon and has the ability to communicate with humans. Its bright intelligence and cunning nature make it stand out in various adventures. Additionally, Meowth values money, which makes it precious. Acting according to its own goals, Meowth has carved out a unique place in the Pokémon world.
